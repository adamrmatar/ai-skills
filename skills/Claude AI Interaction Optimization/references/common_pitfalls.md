Avoid vague instructions like "Be helpful and concise." These do not constrain Claude's responses effectively. Instead, provide detailed guidance.

**Overloading Projects**:
Do not overload projects with irrelevant documents. Keep the knowledge base focused and relevant to avoid confusion.

**Ignoring Memory**:
Regularly review and delete stale memory entries to keep Claude's memory relevant. Ignoring this can lead to outdated or irrelevant context.

**Misusing Adaptive Thinking**:
Choose the appropriate effort level based on the task complexity. Using max effort for simple tasks can waste resources.

**Artifact Errors**:
Ensure that generated artifacts are accurate and functional. Test artifacts thoroughly before sharing.
