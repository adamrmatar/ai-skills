### Core Concepts
Claude AI offers several advanced features that can enhance your interactions:

**Adaptive Thinking**: This feature allows Claude to adjust the amount of effort it puts into each response based on the complexity of the task. You can choose from low, medium, high, or max effort levels.

**Custom Instructions**: Custom instructions guide Claude's responses. They should be specific and detailed, avoiding vague language.

**Projects**: Projects allow you to organize your work into separate workspaces, each with its own knowledge base and instructions. This helps avoid re-explaining context in every new chat.

**Memory**: Memory enables Claude to remember recurring themes and patterns in your conversations. It builds a running summary of your role, projects, and working style.

**Artifacts**: Artifacts are generated outputs such as documents, spreadsheets, and interactive tools. They are stored in a dedicated tab and can be shared with others.

Understanding these core concepts is essential for optimizing your interactions with Claude AI.
