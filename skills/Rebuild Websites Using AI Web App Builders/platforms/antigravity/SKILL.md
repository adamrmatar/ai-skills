## Overview

This skill teaches you how to leverage AI web app builders, specifically Remy from Mind Studio, to rebuild and enhance websites. You'll learn how to guide the AI through the process of creating a professional-level website, including front-end, back-end, database, authentication, and deployment. For more details on the core concepts, see [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow

1. **Define Your Audience and Purpose**: Start by answering questions about your audience and the purpose of your website. This helps the AI understand the context and tailor the build accordingly.

2. **Select AI Categories**: Specify the AI categories your website will cover. This ensures the database is structured to handle new AI tools as they launch.

3. **Guide the AI Through the Build Process**: Use the AI's guided questions to define the front-end, back-end, authentication, and deployment requirements.

4. **Write Copy and Plan User Interaction**: Utilize the AI's capabilities to write website copy and plan for user interaction over time.

5. **Review and Deploy**: Once the AI has built the website, review the final product and deploy it.

For a detailed practical guide, refer to [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md).

## Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: Clearly define your audience and purpose to ensure the AI builds a website that meets your needs.
- **Leverage AI Capabilities**: Use the AI's guided questions to cover all aspects of the website build, from front-end to deployment.
- **Review Thoroughly**: Always review the final product to ensure it meets your standards before deployment.

## Common Pitfalls

- **Vague Responses**: Providing vague answers to the AI's questions can result in a website that doesn't meet your needs.
- **Overlooking User Interaction**: Failing to plan for user interaction can lead to a website that doesn't engage users effectively.

For more on common pitfalls, see [Common Pitfalls](references/common_pitfalls.md).

## Validation and Testing Steps

1. **Functional Testing**: Ensure all website functionalities work as expected.
2. **User Testing**: Conduct user testing to gather feedback on the website's usability and engagement.
3. **Performance Testing**: Test the website's performance to ensure it loads quickly and handles traffic efficiently.
