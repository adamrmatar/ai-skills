## Overview
QuickBooks Workforce integrates payroll and HR functionalities, leveraging AI to streamline processes such as payroll management, recruiting, onboarding, and HR workflows. This skill will guide you through setting up and utilizing these features effectively.

## Core Concepts
- **Payroll AI Agent**: Automates the collection of employee hours and drafts payroll.
- **HR Processes**: Includes recruiting, hiring, onboarding, and HR workflows.
- **Workflow Automation**: Creates repeatable checklists for various HR tasks.

For a deeper understanding, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Set Up Payroll AI Agent**:
   - Navigate to the QuickBooks homepage and click on Feed in the left sidebar.
   - Click on 'See how it works' to access the setup wizard.
   - Set the cadence for reaching out to employees and choose the days of the week.
   - Decide whether to collect hours, overtime, or both.
   - Ensure employees consent to text communications or use the Workforce Mobile app.

2. **Configure Auto Payroll**:
   - Go to the Payroll app under Overview.
   - Click on the Auto Payroll card to configure it.
   - Select employees who are paid the same amount every pay period.

3. **Create Job Postings**:
   - Navigate to the Teams app and click on Recruiting.
   - Fill in job details such as work location, pay details, and job description.
   - Generate a job description if needed.
   - Set up the application form with required documents and screening questions.
   - Publish the job to ZipRecruiter or QuickBooks Workforce.

4. **Onboard New Employees**:
   - Go to all apps, team, and then employees.
   - Click on Add Employee and enter the employee's details.
   - Follow the onboarding steps, including document signing and HR workflows.

5. **Set Up HR Workflows**:
   - Navigate to the team app and click on HR Workflows.
   - Add a template from the library or create a new one.
   - Assign steps to specific team members and customize as needed.
   - Start the workflow for the relevant employee.

For detailed instructions, see [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md).

## Best Practices and Common Pitfalls
- **Best Practices**:
  - Regularly update employee consent for text communications.
  - Use Auto Payroll for employees with consistent pay.
  - Customize HR workflows to fit your company's needs.

- **Common Pitfalls**:
  - Not ensuring employee consent for text communications can lead to compliance issues.
  - Overlooking the customization of HR workflows can result in inefficiencies.

For a comprehensive list, see [Common Pitfalls](references/common_pitfalls.md).

## Validation and Testing Steps
1. Verify that the Payroll AI Agent correctly collects employee hours.
2. Ensure Auto Payroll is configured for the correct employees.
3. Test job postings by applying as a candidate.
4. Validate onboarding steps by simulating a new hire process.
5. Check HR workflows by assigning tasks and ensuring they are completed.
