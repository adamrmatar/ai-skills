# Copilot Instructions: Quickbooks Workforce Payroll And Hr Automation
Description: Automate payroll and HR processes using QuickBooks Workforce, including AI-driven payroll management, recruiting, onboarding, and HR workflows.

## Overview
QuickBooks Workforce integrates payroll and HR functionalities, leveraging AI to streamline processes such as payroll management, recruiting, onboarding, and HR workflows. This skill will guide you through setting up and utilizing these features effectively.

## Core Concepts
- **Payroll AI Agent**: Automates the collection of employee hours and drafts payroll.
- **HR Processes**: Includes recruiting, hiring, onboarding, and HR workflows.
- **Workflow Automation**: Creates repeatable checklists for various HR tasks.

For a deeper understanding, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Set Up Payroll AI Agent**:
   - Navigate to the QuickBooks homepage and click on Feed in the left sidebar.
   - Click on 'See how it works' to access the setup wizard.
   - Set the cadence for reaching out to employees and choose the days of the week.
   - Decide whether to collect hours, overtime, or both.
   - Ensure employees consent to text communications or use the Workforce Mobile app.

2. **Configure Auto Payroll**:
   - Go to the Payroll app under Overview.
   - Click on the Auto Payroll card to configure it.
   - Select employees who are paid the same amount every pay period.

3. **Create Job Postings**:
   - Navigate to the Teams app and click on Recruiting.
   - Fill in job details such as work location, pay details, and job description.
   - Generate a job description if needed.
   - Set up the application form with required documents and screening questions.
   - Publish the job to ZipRecruiter or QuickBooks Workforce.

4. **Onboard New Employees**:
   - Go to all apps, team, and then employees.
   - Click on Add Employee and enter the employee's details.
   - Follow the onboarding steps, including document signing and HR workflows.

5. **Set Up HR Workflows**:
   - Navigate to the team app and click on HR Workflows.
   - Add a template from the library or create a new one.
   - Assign steps to specific team members and customize as needed.
   - Start the workflow for the relevant employee.

For detailed instructions, see [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md).

### Common Pitfalls

# Common Pitfalls

## Payroll AI Agent
- **Employee Consent**: Not ensuring employee consent for text communications can lead to compliance issues. Always verify that employees have agreed to receive texts.
- **Cadence Setting**: Setting an inappropriate cadence for reaching out to employees can result in missed hours or employee frustration. Choose a cadence that aligns with your payroll schedule.

## Auto Payroll
- **Employee Selection**: Incorrectly selecting employees for Auto Payroll can lead to payroll errors. Ensure only employees with consistent pay are included.

## Job Postings
- **Incomplete Details**: Failing to fill in all necessary job details can result in poor candidate quality. Always provide comprehensive information.
- **Screening Questions**: Not setting up relevant screening questions can lead to an influx of unqualified applicants. Tailor questions to the job requirements.

## Onboarding
- **Documentation**: Missing necessary documentation during onboarding can delay the process. Ensure all required documents are prepared in advance.
- **Workflow Assignment**: Incorrectly assigning workflow steps can cause confusion and delays. Double-check assignments before starting the workflow.

### Core Concepts

# Core Concepts

## Payroll AI Agent
The Payroll AI Agent automates the collection of employee hours and drafts payroll. It reaches out to employees via text to gather their hours, ensures the accuracy of the data, and drafts the payroll for approval. This reduces the manual effort required from HR professionals.

## HR Processes
QuickBooks Workforce includes a comprehensive suite of HR functionalities:
- **Recruiting**: Create job postings, manage applications, and screen candidates.
- **Hiring**: Onboard new employees with structured workflows.
- **Onboarding**: Ensure new hires complete necessary documentation and tasks.
- **HR Workflows**: Create repeatable checklists for various HR tasks such as onboarding, terminations, and performance reviews.

## Workflow Automation
Workflow automation allows you to create and manage repeatable checklists for HR tasks. Each workflow consists of steps assigned to specific team members, ensuring that tasks are completed efficiently and consistently.
