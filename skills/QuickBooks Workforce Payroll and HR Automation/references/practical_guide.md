# Practical Guide

## Setting Up the Payroll AI Agent
1. Navigate to the QuickBooks homepage.
2. Click on Feed in the left sidebar.
3. Click on 'See how it works' to access the setup wizard.
4. Set the cadence for reaching out to employees and choose the days of the week.
5. Decide whether to collect hours, overtime, or both.
6. Ensure employees consent to text communications or use the Workforce Mobile app.

## Configuring Auto Payroll
1. Go to the Payroll app under Overview.
2. Click on the Auto Payroll card to configure it.
3. Select employees who are paid the same amount every pay period.

## Creating Job Postings
1. Navigate to the Teams app and click on Recruiting.
2. Fill in job details such as work location, pay details, and job description.
3. Generate a job description if needed.
4. Set up the application form with required documents and screening questions.
5. Publish the job to ZipRecruiter or QuickBooks Workforce.

## Onboarding New Employees
1. Go to all apps, team, and then employees.
2. Click on Add Employee and enter the employee's details.
3. Follow the onboarding steps, including document signing and HR workflows.

## Setting Up HR Workflows
1. Navigate to the team app and click on HR Workflows.
2. Add a template from the library or create a new one.
3. Assign steps to specific team members and customize as needed.
4. Start the workflow for the relevant employee.
