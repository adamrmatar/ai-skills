# Practical Guide
This guide provides practical steps and tips for creating AI videos using free AI tools. Follow these steps to streamline your video creation process.

## Step 1: Generate a Story
Start by generating a story for your video. Use the magic tool to input an idea, provide a YouTube link, or select a theme. Ensure your input is clear and concise to get the best results.

## Step 2: Select Video Style
Choose the style of your video. Options include realistic, cartoon, and documentary. Select the style that best fits your story.

## Step 3: Set Video Duration
Specify the duration of your video. You can select from predefined options or enter a custom duration.

## Step 4: Review Script
Review the generated script and confirm if it meets your requirements. If not, request a new story.

## Step 5: Divide into Scenes
The tool will divide the story into scenes. Review and confirm the division. Adjust if necessary.

## Step 6: Create Images
Use Chat GPT to create images for each scene. Generate visuals that match the scene's description.

## Step 7: Convert Images to Videos
Upload the images to Flow AI and use the provided prompts to convert them into video clips. Choose appropriate quality settings to balance cost and video quality.

## Step 8: Assemble Video
Combine the video clips within Flow AI to create the final video. Preview each clip and ensure dialogues and lip-syncing are accurate.

Following these steps will help you create high-quality AI videos efficiently.
