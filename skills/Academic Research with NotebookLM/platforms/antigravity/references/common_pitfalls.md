# Common Pitfalls in Academic Research with NotebookLM

While NotebookLM is a powerful tool, there are some common pitfalls to avoid to ensure effective use in academic research.

## Overloading Sources
Avoid uploading too many irrelevant sources. Focus on quality over quantity to keep your research focused and manageable.

## Ignoring Gaps
Don't just focus on what's present in your sources. Look for what's missing to identify research gaps and guide your focus.

## Inaccurate Literature Reviews
Cross-check the generated literature review with your original sources to ensure accuracy and completeness.

## Lack of Self-Critique
Failing to critique your own writing can lead to unsupported claims and logical gaps. Use NotebookLM to identify and address these issues.

## Poor Data Visualization
Ensure that the data tables and visual aids you create accurately represent your findings. Misleading visuals can detract from your research.

By avoiding these pitfalls, you can leverage NotebookLM more effectively in your academic research.
