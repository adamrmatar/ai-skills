# Claude Code Custom Instructions - Academic Research Workflow Automation With Consensus Ai And Claude
> This skill enables you to automate academic research workflows by integrating Consensus AI with Claude. It includes connecting Consensus AI, uploading and creating custom skills, and executing complex research tasks such as literature reviews, grant research, and identifying research gaps.

### Overview
This skill allows you to leverage the power of Consensus AI and Claude to automate and enhance academic research workflows. By connecting Consensus AI to Claude, you can access peer-reviewed papers, build search strategies, generate structured outputs, and run pre-built skills such as literature review helpers and grant research tools. For a deeper understanding of the core concepts, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

### Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Connect Consensus AI to Claude**:
   - Open Claude and navigate to the customization section.
   - Click on 'Browse Connectors' and search for Consensus AI.
   - Click on Consensus AI and follow the connection process, including signing in.

2. **Upload Pre-Built Skills**:
   - Download the desired skill (e.g., literature review helper) from the Consensus AI website.
   - In Claude, go to the skills section and upload the downloaded skill.

3. **Create Custom Skills**:
   - Use Claude's skill creator to develop custom skills tailored to your specific research needs.
   - Follow the prompts to define the processes and outcomes you want the skill to achieve.

4. **Execute Research Tasks**:
   - In Claude's chat function, ensure that the Consensus AI connector is selected.
   - Import the skills you want to use.
   - Enter your research query (e.g., 'Find research gaps for my PhD project on OPV devices').
   - Provide any necessary clarifications or adjustments as prompted.

5. **Review and Utilize Outputs**:
   - Claude will generate a detailed report based on the Consensus AI data and the skills used.
   - Review the outputs, which may include topic overviews, priority reading orders, key papers, and identified research gaps.

### Best Practices
- **Ensure Proper Connection**: Always verify that Consensus AI is properly connected to Claude before starting any research tasks.
- **Use Detailed Prompts**: Provide as much detail as possible in your prompts to get the most accurate and relevant outputs.
- **Review Outputs Thoroughly**: Carefully review the generated reports to ensure they meet your research needs.

### Common Pitfalls
- **Incomplete Connections**: Failing to properly connect Consensus AI can result in incomplete or inaccurate data retrieval.
- **Vague Prompts**: Using vague prompts can lead to irrelevant or incomplete outputs. Always specify the depth and focus of your research.

### Validation and Testing
- **Test Connections**: Ensure that Consensus AI is correctly connected by running a simple query.
- **Validate Outputs**: Cross-check the generated outputs with known academic sources to verify accuracy.
- **Iterate and Refine**: Use feedback from initial runs to refine your skills and prompts for better results.

## Introduction
This document outlines the core concepts behind integrating Consensus AI with Claude to automate academic research workflows. Understanding these concepts is crucial for effectively leveraging the combined power of these tools.

## Key Concepts

### Consensus AI
Consensus AI is an academic AI tool that provides access to peer-reviewed papers and generates structured data for research purposes. It enhances the capabilities of large language models like Claude by providing specialized academic content.

### Claude
Claude is a large language model that can execute complex workflows and generate detailed outputs based on user prompts. By integrating Consensus AI, Claude can perform specialized academic tasks such as literature reviews and grant research.

### Skills
Skills are predefined workflows or processes that can be uploaded or created within Claude. These skills automate specific academic tasks, such as literature reviews or identifying research gaps.

### Connectors
Connectors are the interfaces that link external tools like Consensus AI to Claude. Properly configuring these connectors is essential for seamless data retrieval and task execution.

## Practical Applications

### Literature Reviews
Using Consensus AI and Claude, you can automate the process of conducting literature reviews. This includes identifying key papers, summarizing findings, and highlighting research gaps.

### Grant Research
Automate the process of researching grants by using Consensus AI to identify relevant funding opportunities and Claude to generate structured outputs.

### Identifying Research Gaps
