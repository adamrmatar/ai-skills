# Practical Guide

## Introduction
This guide provides step-by-step instructions for integrating Consensus AI with Claude and executing academic research workflows. Follow these steps to maximize the efficiency and accuracy of your research tasks.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Connecting Consensus AI to Claude
1. Open Claude and navigate to the customization section.
2. Click on 'Browse Connectors' and search for Consensus AI.
3. Click on Consensus AI and follow the connection process, including signing in.

### Uploading Pre-Built Skills
1. Download the desired skill (e.g., literature review helper) from the Consensus AI website.
2. In Claude, go to the skills section and upload the downloaded skill.

### Creating Custom Skills
1. Use Claude's skill creator to develop custom skills tailored to your specific research needs.
2. Follow the prompts to define the processes and outcomes you want the skill to achieve.

### Executing Research Tasks
1. In Claude's chat function, ensure that the Consensus AI connector is selected.
2. Import the skills you want to use.
3. Enter your research query (e.g., 'Find research gaps for my PhD project on OPV devices').
4. Provide any necessary clarifications or adjustments as prompted.

### Reviewing and Utilizing Outputs
1. Claude will generate a detailed report based on the Consensus AI data and the skills used.
2. Review the outputs, which may include topic overviews, priority reading orders, key papers, and identified research gaps.

## Best Practices

### Ensure Proper Connection
Always verify that Consensus AI is properly connected to Claude before starting any research tasks.

### Use Detailed Prompts
Provide as much detail as possible in your prompts to get the most accurate and relevant outputs.

### Review Outputs Thoroughly
Carefully review the generated reports to ensure they meet your research needs.

## Common Pitfalls

### Incomplete Connections
Failing to properly connect Consensus AI can result in incomplete or inaccurate data retrieval.

### Vague Prompts
Using vague prompts can lead to irrelevant or incomplete outputs. Always specify the depth and focus of your research.

## Conclusion
By following this practical guide, you can effectively integrate Consensus AI with Claude, automating and enhancing your academic research workflows.
