# Core Concepts

## Introduction
This document outlines the core concepts behind integrating Consensus AI with Claude to automate academic research workflows. Understanding these concepts is crucial for effectively leveraging the combined power of these tools.

## Key Concepts

### Consensus AI
Consensus AI is an academic AI tool that provides access to peer-reviewed papers and generates structured data for research purposes. It enhances the capabilities of large language models like Claude by providing specialized academic content.

### Claude
Claude is a large language model that can execute complex workflows and generate detailed outputs based on user prompts. By integrating Consensus AI, Claude can perform specialized academic tasks such as literature reviews and grant research.

### Skills
Skills are predefined workflows or processes that can be uploaded or created within Claude. These skills automate specific academic tasks, such as literature reviews or identifying research gaps.

### Connectors
Connectors are the interfaces that link external tools like Consensus AI to Claude. Properly configuring these connectors is essential for seamless data retrieval and task execution.

## Practical Applications

### Literature Reviews
Using Consensus AI and Claude, you can automate the process of conducting literature reviews. This includes identifying key papers, summarizing findings, and highlighting research gaps.

### Grant Research
Automate the process of researching grants by using Consensus AI to identify relevant funding opportunities and Claude to generate structured outputs.

### Identifying Research Gaps
Leverage the combined power of Consensus AI and Claude to identify gaps in existing research, providing valuable insights for your academic projects.

## Conclusion
Understanding these core concepts will enable you to effectively integrate Consensus AI with Claude, automating and enhancing your academic research workflows.
