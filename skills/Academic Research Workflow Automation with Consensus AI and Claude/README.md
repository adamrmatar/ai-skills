# Academic Research Workflow Automation With Consensus Ai And Claude

This skill enables you to automate academic research workflows by integrating Consensus AI with Claude. It includes connecting Consensus AI, uploading and creating custom skills, and executing complex research tasks such as literature reviews, grant research, and identifying research gaps.

## Setup & Installation

This skill is agent-agnostic and packaged for multiple developer agents. Find your target platform below:

### 💻 Antigravity
_Copy the contents of `platforms/antigravity/` to `~/.gemini/config/skills/Academic Research Workflow Automation with Consensus AI and Claude/`._

### 💻 Cursor
_Copy `platforms/cursor/Academic Research Workflow Automation with Consensus AI and Claude.mdc` to your project's `.cursor/rules/` directory._

### 💻 Claude
_Append the contents of `platforms/claude/CLAUDE.md` to the target project's `CLAUDE.md` file._

### 💻 Copilot
_Copy `platforms/copilot/Academic Research Workflow Automation with Consensus AI and Claude.instructions.md` to your project's `.github/instructions/` directory._

### 💻 Windsurf
_Append the contents of `platforms/windsurf/.windsurfrules` to the target project's `.windsurfrules` file._
