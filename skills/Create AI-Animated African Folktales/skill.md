## Overview
This skill teaches you how to create AI-animated African folktales, leveraging AI tools for script generation, character design, and video production. The process involves generating a story, creating visuals, and editing the final video. For a deeper understanding of the core concepts, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Generate the Story Script**: Use an AI tool like ChatGPT to create a compelling story. Example prompt: `"Write me a short story of two glamorous Nigerian ladies with taste of class who s for money in wrong places and ended up in madness in a pizza style with narrator's voice."`
2. **Refine the Script**: Copy the generated script into an editing app like Microsoft Word or WordPad. Refine it to remove robotic language and add cultural elements.
3. **Create Character Descriptions**: Generate detailed descriptions of the main characters. Example prompt: `"Write the main character's description."`
4. **Generate Visuals**: Use text-to-image AI tools like Whisk AI to create visuals based on the story paragraphs. Example prompt: `"Write text to image prompt of each paragraph of the story in pizza style."`
5. **Assemble the Video**: Use video editing software like CapCut to assemble the visuals and voiceover. Ensure the visuals align with the voiceover.
6. **Add Animations**: Animate the visuals to make the video more engaging. Use tools within CapCut to add animations.
7. **Finalize and Export**: Review the video, make necessary adjustments, and export the final product.

## Best Practices
- **Refine the Script**: Always refine the AI-generated script to add cultural elements and remove robotic language.
- **Consistent Characters**: Use reference images to maintain consistent character appearances throughout the video.
- **Natural Voiceover**: Use your own voice for the voiceover to avoid robotic content and potential issues with platforms like YouTube.

## Common Pitfalls
- **Robotic Scripts**: Failing to refine the AI-generated script can result in robotic and unnatural storytelling.
- **Inconsistent Characters**: Not using reference images can lead to inconsistent character appearances.
- **Overuse of AI Voiceovers**: Using AI voiceovers can make the content feel robotic and may lead to issues with content platforms.

## Validation Steps
1. **Script Review**: Ensure the script is culturally relevant and free of robotic language.
2. **Visual Consistency**: Check that the character visuals are consistent throughout the video.
3. **Voiceover Alignment**: Verify that the voiceover aligns with the visuals and enhances the storytelling.
4. **Final Review**: Watch the final video to ensure it meets quality standards and effectively tells the story.
