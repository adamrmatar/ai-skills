## Common Pitfalls in AI-Powered Document Creation with Adobe Acrobat

While Adobe Acrobat's AI capabilities offer powerful tools for document creation and management, there are common pitfalls to be aware of. Understanding these pitfalls can help you avoid mistakes and maximize the effectiveness of your workflows.

### Overloading the PDF Space
One common mistake is adding too many files to the PDF space at once. This can overwhelm the AI assistant and result in less accurate summaries and insights. To avoid this, organize your files and add them in manageable batches.

### Vague Prompts
Using vague prompts can lead to irrelevant or incomplete outputs. For example, a prompt like "Create a document" is too broad and may not generate useful results. Instead, use clear and specific prompts to guide the AI assistant. For example, "Create a detailed first week checklist for new employees based on the onboarding materials" provides clear direction.

### Ignoring Citations
The AI assistant provides citations for the information it generates. Ignoring these citations can lead to missed opportunities for further exploration and refinement. Always review the citations to ensure they reference the correct source documents and use them to refine your content.

### Not Reviewing AI-Generated Content
While the AI assistant can generate high-quality content, it's essential to review and edit the outputs to ensure they meet your requirements and maintain a professional tone. Skipping this step can result in documents that don't fully align with your goals.

### Overlooking Templates
Adobe Express templates provide professionally designed layouts for your documents. Overlooking these templates can result in documents that lack visual appeal and professionalism. Always explore and use templates to enhance your documents.

### Best Practices to Avoid Pitfalls
- **Organize Your Files**: Ensure your files are well-organized before adding them to the PDF space.
- **Use Clear Prompts**: When creating new documents, use clear and specific prompts to guide the AI assistant.
- **Review Citations**: Always review the citations provided by the AI assistant to ensure they reference the correct source documents.
- **Edit AI-Generated Content**: Review and edit the AI-generated content to ensure it meets your requirements and maintains a professional tone.
- **Use Templates**: Enhance your documents by using professionally designed templates from Adobe Express.

By being aware of these common pitfalls and following best practices, you can effectively use Adobe Acrobat's AI capabilities to streamline your document workflows and create polished, professional documents.
