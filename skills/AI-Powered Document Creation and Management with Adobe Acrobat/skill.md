## Overview
Adobe Acrobat has evolved into a powerful AI-driven workspace for document creation and management. By leveraging its AI capabilities, you can combine multiple file types, generate summaries, create new documents, and repurpose content efficiently. This skill will guide you through the process of using Adobe Acrobat's AI tools to streamline your document workflows.

## Core Concepts
Before diving into the workflow, it's essential to understand the core concepts of Adobe Acrobat's AI-driven document creation. These include PDF spaces, AI assistant, and Adobe Express templates. For a detailed explanation, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Create a PDF Space**: Start by creating a new PDF space in Adobe Acrobat. This space will serve as your workspace for combining and managing documents.
2. **Select Files**: Choose files from your device, cloud storage, or recent files. Adobe Acrobat supports various file types, including PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoint presentations, and video transcripts.
3. **Add Files to PDF Space**: Add the selected files to your PDF space. Adobe Acrobat will analyze the files and generate a summary.
4. **Generate Summaries and Insights**: Use the AI assistant to generate summaries and insights from the combined documents. This step helps you understand the content and identify key points.
5. **Create New Documents**: Use AI-driven prompts to create new documents based on the content in your PDF space. For example, you can generate a checklist, presentation, or welcome letter.
6. **Repurpose Content**: Repurpose existing content by asking the AI assistant for creative ideas. This step allows you to explore new ways to use your content.
7. **Use Templates**: Enhance your documents by using professionally designed templates from Adobe Express. These templates provide a polished and professional finish to your documents.

## Best Practices and Common Pitfalls
### Best Practices
- **Organize Your Files**: Ensure your files are well-organized before adding them to the PDF space. This will make it easier for the AI assistant to analyze and generate accurate summaries.
- **Use Clear Prompts**: When creating new documents, use clear and specific prompts to guide the AI assistant. This will result in more relevant and useful outputs.
- **Review and Edit**: Always review and edit the AI-generated content to ensure it meets your requirements and maintains a professional tone.

### Common Pitfalls
- **Overloading the PDF Space**: Avoid adding too many files to the PDF space at once. This can overwhelm the AI assistant and result in less accurate summaries.
- **Vague Prompts**: Using vague prompts can lead to irrelevant or incomplete outputs. Be specific about what you want the AI assistant to generate.
- **Ignoring Citations**: The AI assistant provides citations for the information it generates. Ignoring these citations can lead to missed opportunities for further exploration and refinement.

For more detailed guidelines and examples, refer to [Common Pitfalls](references/common_pitfalls.md).

## Validation and Testing Steps
1. **Check Summaries**: Verify that the summaries generated by the AI assistant accurately reflect the content of the combined documents.
2. **Review Outputs**: Review the AI-generated documents to ensure they meet your requirements and maintain a professional tone.
3. **Test Templates**: Test different Adobe Express templates to ensure they enhance the visual appeal and professionalism of your documents.
4. **Validate Citations**: Check the citations provided by the AI assistant to ensure they reference the correct source documents.
