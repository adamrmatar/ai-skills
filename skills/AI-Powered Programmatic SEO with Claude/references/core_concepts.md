# Core Concepts

## Topic Patterns
Topic patterns are repeatable keyword structures that can be scaled across hundreds of pages. They are generated based on your niche and the product or goal you are promoting. These patterns help in creating a consistent content strategy.

## Page Templates
Page templates are blueprints for creating SEO-optimized content. They must include:
1. **Unique Angle**: What makes your content different?
2. **Fresh Data**: Use Claude and web search to generate validated content.
3. **SEO and AIO Optimization**: Ensure content is optimized for both search engines and AI models.
4. **Goal Blending**: Each page should funnel towards your goal.

## Claude Skills
Claude skills are reusable systems that automate content generation. They allow you to generate pages based on specific patterns and templates, ensuring consistency and scalability.

## Programmatic SEO
Programmatic SEO involves automating the creation of SEO-optimized content at scale. With Claude, this process becomes AI-powered, allowing for efficient and effective content generation.
