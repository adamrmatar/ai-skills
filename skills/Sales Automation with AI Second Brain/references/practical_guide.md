## Practical Guide
### Setting Up Your Sales Second Brain
1. **Create Folder Structure**: Start by creating a folder structure for your sales second brain. Use subfolders to organize data by categories like calls, deals, and lead gen campaigns.
2. **Use Obsidian**: Use Obsidian to visualize and manage your folder structure. Obsidian helps you see connections between different documents and makes it easier to navigate your second brain.

### Integrating CRM and Sales Tools
1. **Connect CRM**: Connect your CRM to your AI system using connectors. Ensure data flows seamlessly into your second brain.
2. **Add Sales Tools**: Integrate other sales tools like meeting transcription tools, proposal generators, and outreach platforms.

### Creating Scheduled Tasks
1. **Morning Routine**: Set up a morning routine to update your second brain with the latest sales intelligence. This includes pulling calendar events, prepping calls, and logging meeting transcripts.
2. **CRM Sync**: Create a CRM sync task to ensure your CRM is up-to-date and the status of each lead is correct.
3. **Sales Call Scoring**: Set up a sales call scoring task to grade the performance of sales reps on calls.

### Building Personalized Dashboards
1. **Create Dashboard**: Use tools like CloudCode and Vercel to create personalized sales dashboards. Ensure the dashboard displays the latest sales intelligence.
2. **Update Daily**: Set up a scheduled task to update the dashboard daily with the latest information.
