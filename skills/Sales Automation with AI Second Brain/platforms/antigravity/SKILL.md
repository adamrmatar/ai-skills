## Overview
This skill teaches you how to automate sales processes using an AI-powered second brain system. The system integrates CRM, sales intelligence, and personalized dashboards to streamline lead generation, call prep, CRM updates, follow-ups, and analytics. For a deeper understanding of the core concepts, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Set Up Your Sales Second Brain**: Create a folder structure for your sales second brain. Use tools like Obsidian to visualize and manage the folder. Refer to [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md) for detailed instructions.
2. **Integrate CRM and Sales Tools**: Connect your CRM and other sales tools to your AI system. Use connectors to ensure data flows seamlessly into your second brain.
3. **Create Scheduled Tasks**: Set up scheduled tasks or routines to update your second brain daily. Use prompts to automate tasks like call prep, CRM sync, and sales call scoring. See [Code Examples](references/code_examples.md) for sample prompts.
4. **Build Personalized Dashboards**: Create personalized sales dashboards using tools like CloudCode and Vercel. Ensure the dashboard is updated daily with the latest sales intelligence.
5. **Automate Repetitive Tasks**: Use AI skills to automate repetitive sales tasks like lead generation, outreach, and follow-ups. Customize these skills based on your specific needs.

## Best Practices and Common Pitfalls
- **Best Practice**: Regularly update your second brain with the latest sales intelligence to ensure accurate and relevant outputs.
- **Common Pitfall**: Neglecting to set up scheduled tasks can lead to outdated information in your second brain. Always ensure tasks are automated and running daily.

## Validation and Testing Steps
1. **Verify Data Flow**: Ensure data from your CRM and sales tools is correctly flowing into your second brain.
2. **Test Scheduled Tasks**: Run scheduled tasks manually to verify they update the second brain as expected.
3. **Review Dashboard**: Check your personalized dashboard to ensure it displays the latest sales intelligence.

For more detailed validation steps, refer to [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md).
