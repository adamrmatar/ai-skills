## Core Concepts
### AI Second Brain
An AI second brain is a system that stores and organizes sales intelligence, making it accessible to AI tools for automation. It acts as a central repository for all sales-related data, including CRM updates, meeting transcripts, and lead information.

### Sales Intelligence
Sales intelligence refers to the data and insights gathered from various sales activities. This includes lead research, ICP criteria, sales metrics, and meeting transcripts. Accurate and up-to-date sales intelligence is crucial for effective sales automation.

### Personalized Dashboards
Personalized dashboards provide a centralized view of all relevant sales intelligence. They help sales reps and managers focus on the most important tasks and leads each day. Dashboards can be customized to show metrics like pipeline trends, meetings booked, and revenue per week.

### Scheduled Tasks
Scheduled tasks are automated routines that update the second brain with the latest sales intelligence. These tasks ensure that the second brain remains up-to-date and provides accurate information for AI tools to automate sales processes.
