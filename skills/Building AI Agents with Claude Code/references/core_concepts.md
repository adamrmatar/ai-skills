# Core Concepts

## AI Harness
Claude Code acts as a harness that leverages AI models like Opus, Sonnet, or Haiku to perform tasks. Think of it as the engine that powers your AI agent. The harness allows your AI agent to access local files, perform web searches, and interact with online services like Gmail, Slack, and CRM.

## Context Engineering
Context engineering is the process of feeding relevant context to your AI agent to improve its output. This includes providing meeting transcripts, YouTube videos, Slack threads, and other relevant data. The more context your AI agent has, the better it can perform its tasks.

## Iterative Improvement
Iterative improvement involves continuously refining your AI agent based on feedback and performance data. This process ensures that your AI agent becomes more effective over time. Regularly review the outputs of your AI agent and make adjustments as needed.

## Practical Applications
Claude Code can be used for a variety of tasks, including organizing files, drafting emails, analyzing data, and automating complex workflows. By leveraging the power of AI models and context engineering, you can create AI agents that perform tasks more efficiently and effectively.
