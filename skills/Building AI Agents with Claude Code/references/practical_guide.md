# Practical Guide

## Installation and Setup
1. **Download Claude Code**: Visit the Claude Code website and download the desktop app or use it within VS Code.
2. **Sign In**: Sign in to your Claude Code account using your credentials.
3. **Configure Access**: Set up Claude Code to access your local files and online services like Gmail, Slack, and CRM.

## Creating Your First AI Agent
1. **Start Simple**: Begin with a straightforward task, such as organizing your downloads folder.
2. **Use Prompts**: Use prompts to instruct your AI agent. For example, "Hey Claude, please organize my downloads folder by file type and move them into appropriate subfolders."
3. **Review Outputs**: Review the outputs of your AI agent to ensure it performed the task correctly.

## Context Engineering
1. **Feed Relevant Context**: Provide your AI agent with relevant context, such as meeting transcripts, YouTube videos, and Slack threads.
2. **Use Examples**: Use examples to guide your AI agent. For example, "Hey Claude, draft a professional email to a customer who has inquired about our pricing plans. Use a friendly tone and include a call to action."
3. **Iterate**: Continuously refine the context you provide to improve your AI agent's performance.

## Iterative Improvement
1. **Gather Feedback**: Collect feedback from users or stakeholders to identify areas for improvement.
2. **Refine Prompts**: Use the feedback to refine the prompts you use with your AI agent.
3. **Test and Validate**: Regularly test and validate your AI agent to ensure it meets your expectations.
