# Common Pitfalls

## Overlooking Context
Failing to provide sufficient context can lead to generic or irrelevant outputs. Always ensure your AI agent has the necessary context to perform its tasks effectively.

## Ignoring Feedback
Not using feedback to improve your AI agent can result in stagnant performance. Regularly review the outputs of your AI agent and make adjustments as needed.

## Scope Creep
Adding too many features at once can complicate your AI agent and reduce its effectiveness. Start with simple tasks and gradually add complexity as needed.

## Trusting Outputs Blindly
As AI gets better, it gets easier to trust the first output it gives you. Always review and validate the outputs of your AI agent to ensure they meet your expectations.

## Not Iterating
Iterative improvement is crucial for optimizing your AI agent's performance. Continuously refine your AI agent based on feedback and performance data.
