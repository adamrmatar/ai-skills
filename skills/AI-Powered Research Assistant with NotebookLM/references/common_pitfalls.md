# Common Pitfalls
Here are some common pitfalls to avoid when using NotebookLM.

### Overloading Sources
Avoid adding too many sources at once, which can make it difficult to manage and analyze.

### Ignoring Citations
Always check the citations provided by NotebookLM to ensure the accuracy of the information.

### Not Verifying Outputs
Always verify important information and outputs, especially for work or research.

### Poor Organization
Use auto-tagging to keep your sources organized and avoid confusion.

### Not Saving Insights
Save important insights as notes for easy reference and future use.
