# Practical Guide
This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to use NotebookLM effectively.

### Step 1: Access NotebookLM
Go to the NotebookLM website and sign in with your Google account.

### Step 2: Create a New Notebook
Click on 'Create New' in the top right corner to start a new notebook.

### Step 3: Name Your Notebook
Give your notebook a descriptive name related to your project or topic.

### Step 4: Add Sources
Click on 'Add Sources' to include PDFs, notes, websites, and videos. You can drag and drop files or select them from Google Drive.

### Step 5: Organize Sources
Use the auto-tagging feature to group similar sources together for better organization.

### Step 6: Analyze Content
Use the chat panel to ask questions and get summaries or insights from your sources.

### Step 7: Generate Content
Use the studio to create mind maps, audio overviews, and other content based on your sources.

### Step 8: Save and Share
Save important insights as notes and share your notebook with others by clicking on 'Share' in the top right corner.

### Tips
- **Verify Information**: Always verify important information and outputs, especially for work or research.
- **Organize Sources**: Use auto-tagging to keep your sources organized.
- **Save Insights**: Save important insights as notes for easy reference.
