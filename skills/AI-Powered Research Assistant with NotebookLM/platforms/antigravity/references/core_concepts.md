# Core Concepts
NotebookLM is an AI-powered research assistant that helps you transform various types of content into actionable insights. It supports PDFs, notes, websites, and videos, allowing you to summarize, analyze, and generate content based on your sources.

### Key Features
- **Summarization**: Automatically generates summaries of your documents.
- **Question Answering**: Answers questions based on the content of your sources.
- **Mind Maps**: Creates visual maps showing how different ideas and topics connect.
- **Audio Overviews**: Generates AI-generated podcasts based on your sources.

### Use Cases
- **Research**: Quickly gather and analyze information from multiple sources.
- **Content Creation**: Generate mind maps, podcasts, and reports based on your research.
- **Organization**: Keep your sources and insights organized with auto-tagging and notes.

### Getting Started
To start using NotebookLM, you need a Google account and access to the NotebookLM website. Once signed in, you can create a new notebook and begin adding sources.
