## Overview
This skill enables you to integrate and utilize QuickBooks AI agents to automate various business processes, including accounting, tax optimization, payment processing, lead management, and project management. For a detailed understanding of the core concepts, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Access the Business Feed**: Navigate to the Home Screen and click on the Feed icon in the left sidebar to view AI agent recommendations.
2. **Review Tax Deductions**: Click on the Maximize your deductions card to explore commonly missed deductions. For practical steps, see [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md).
3. **Categorize Expenses**: Use the Accounting AI to review and post transactions marked as ready. Always verify vendor names.
4. **Reconcile Accounts**: Upload your bank statement and let the AI match transactions. Resolve any discrepancies highlighted.
5. **Process Payments**: Set up late fees and use the invoice autofill tool to generate invoices from contracts or emails.
6. **Manage Leads**: Connect your email to sync leads and use the Customer AI to draft follow-up emails.
7. **Set Up Projects**: Upload project documents to the Project Management AI to auto-generate timelines and milestones.

# Detailed Guidelines

## Common Pitfalls

# Common Pitfalls and Best Practices

## Pitfalls
1. **Over-reliance on AI**: The Business Tax AI is not a substitute for a certified accountant. Always verify deductions with a professional.
2. **Vendor Accuracy**: The Accounting AI may mislabel vendors even if categories are correct. Always double-check vendor names.
3. **Lead Sync Delay**: New leads take up to 24 hours to sync with the Customer AI. Do not rely on it for instant follow-ups.
4. **Invoice Errors**: Autofilled invoices may have incorrect quantities or dates. Always review line items before sending.

## Best Practices
1. **Regular Reviews**: Schedule weekly reviews of AI-suggested transactions and deductions to ensure accuracy.
2. **Training the AI**: Use the Customer AI's training feature to improve lead scoring and email drafts by inputting business-specific information.
3. **Documentation**: Keep all project documents and contracts organized for seamless use with the Project Management AI.
4. **Legal Compliance**: Verify local laws before applying late fees or sales tax suggestions to avoid legal issues.

By avoiding these pitfalls and adhering to best practices, you can effectively leverage QuickBooks AI agents to enhance your business operations.

## Core Concepts

# QuickBooks AI Agents: Core Concepts

QuickBooks Online offers a suite of AI agents designed to automate various business processes. These agents are tiered based on your subscription plan, with more advanced features available in higher-tier plans. The core agents include:

1. **Base AI**: Available on all plans, includes AI chat, Business Tax AI for maximizing deductions, and Accounting AI for automatic expense categorization.
2. **Essentials Plan AI**: Adds full Accounting AI for batch expense categorization and Payments AI to expedite payments.
3. **Plus Plan AI**: Introduces AI-powered reconciliation, Sales Tax AI, and Customer AI for lead tracking.
4. **Advanced Plan AI**: Includes Finance AI for executive-level reporting and Project Management AI for automating project setup.

Each agent operates within its domain, providing recommendations and automating tasks while requiring user approval for final actions. For example, the Business Tax AI identifies commonly missed deductions but advises consulting a certified accountant before finalizing claims. Similarly, the Accounting AI categorizes expenses but leaves vendor verification to the user.

Understanding these core concepts is essential for effectively leveraging QuickBooks AI agents to streamline your business operations.

## Practical Guide

# Practical Guide to Using QuickBooks AI Agents

## Business Tax AI
1. **Access**: Navigate to the Business Feed and click on the Maximize your deductions card.
2. **Review**: Scroll through the list of commonly missed deductions specific to your industry.
3. **Add Expense**: Click on any relevant deduction to open Intuit Intelligence Chat and record the expense.

## Accounting AI
1. **Auto Categorization**: Look for exclamation or check marks next to uncategorized expenses to see AI suggestions.
2. **Batch Posting**: On Essentials and higher plans, review and post multiple transactions marked as ready by the AI.
3. **Reconciliation**: Upload your bank statement to let the AI match transactions and highlight discrepancies.

## Payments AI
1. **Late Fees**: Set up late fee policies directly from the feed notifications.
2. **Invoice Autofill**: Copy and paste contract details or emails into the invoice autofill tool to generate invoices automatically.

## Customer AI
1. **Lead Tracking**: Connect your email to sync leads, which are scored as warm, hot, or cold.
2. **Draft Responses**: Use the AI to draft follow-up emails based on lead interactions.

## Project Management AI
1. **Project Setup**: Upload project documents to automatically generate timelines and milestones.
2. **Task Management**: On Enterprise plans, tasks are also auto-filled from project documents.

This guide provides actionable steps to maximize the utility of each AI agent within QuickBooks Online.
