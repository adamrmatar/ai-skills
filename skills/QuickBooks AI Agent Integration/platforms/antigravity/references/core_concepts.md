# QuickBooks AI Agents: Core Concepts

QuickBooks Online offers a suite of AI agents designed to automate various business processes. These agents are tiered based on your subscription plan, with more advanced features available in higher-tier plans. The core agents include:

1. **Base AI**: Available on all plans, includes AI chat, Business Tax AI for maximizing deductions, and Accounting AI for automatic expense categorization.
2. **Essentials Plan AI**: Adds full Accounting AI for batch expense categorization and Payments AI to expedite payments.
3. **Plus Plan AI**: Introduces AI-powered reconciliation, Sales Tax AI, and Customer AI for lead tracking.
4. **Advanced Plan AI**: Includes Finance AI for executive-level reporting and Project Management AI for automating project setup.

Each agent operates within its domain, providing recommendations and automating tasks while requiring user approval for final actions. For example, the Business Tax AI identifies commonly missed deductions but advises consulting a certified accountant before finalizing claims. Similarly, the Accounting AI categorizes expenses but leaves vendor verification to the user.

Understanding these core concepts is essential for effectively leveraging QuickBooks AI agents to streamline your business operations.
