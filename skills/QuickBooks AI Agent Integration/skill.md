# QuickBooks AI Agent Integration Skill

## Overview
This skill enables you to integrate and utilize QuickBooks AI agents to automate various business processes, including accounting, tax optimization, payment processing, lead management, and project management. For a detailed understanding of the core concepts, refer to [Core Concepts](references/core_concepts.md).

## Step-by-Step Workflow
1. **Access the Business Feed**: Navigate to the Home Screen and click on the Feed icon in the left sidebar to view AI agent recommendations.
2. **Review Tax Deductions**: Click on the Maximize your deductions card to explore commonly missed deductions. For practical steps, see [Practical Guide](references/practical_guide.md).
3. **Categorize Expenses**: Use the Accounting AI to review and post transactions marked as ready. Always verify vendor names.
4. **Reconcile Accounts**: Upload your bank statement and let the AI match transactions. Resolve any discrepancies highlighted.
5. **Process Payments**: Set up late fees and use the invoice autofill tool to generate invoices from contracts or emails.
6. **Manage Leads**: Connect your email to sync leads and use the Customer AI to draft follow-up emails.
7. **Set Up Projects**: Upload project documents to the Project Management AI to auto-generate timelines and milestones.

## Code/Prompt Templates
```plaintext
Invoice Autofill Prompt:
1. Copy the entire email or contract text.
2. Paste it into the invoice autofill box.
3. Click Autofill to generate the invoice.
4. Review and correct any errors before sending.
```

## Best Practices
- Regularly review AI suggestions for accuracy.
- Train the Customer AI with business-specific information to improve lead scoring and email drafts.
- Keep project documents organized for seamless use with the Project Management AI.

## Common Pitfalls
- Over-relying on AI without professional verification.
- Ignoring vendor name accuracy in categorized transactions.
- Expecting instant lead sync with the Customer AI.
For more pitfalls and how to avoid them, see [Common Pitfalls](references/common_pitfalls.md).

## Validation Steps
1. Verify that all AI-suggested deductions are accurate and applicable.
2. Check that categorized expenses have correct vendors and categories.
3. Ensure reconciled accounts have zero discrepancies.
4. Confirm that autofilled invoices have correct quantities and dates.
5. Test lead follow-up emails for relevance and professionalism.

By following this skill, you can effectively automate and optimize your business processes using QuickBooks AI agents.
