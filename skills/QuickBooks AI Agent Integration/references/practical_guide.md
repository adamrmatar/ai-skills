# Practical Guide to Using QuickBooks AI Agents

## Business Tax AI
1. **Access**: Navigate to the Business Feed and click on the Maximize your deductions card.
2. **Review**: Scroll through the list of commonly missed deductions specific to your industry.
3. **Add Expense**: Click on any relevant deduction to open Intuit Intelligence Chat and record the expense.

## Accounting AI
1. **Auto Categorization**: Look for exclamation or check marks next to uncategorized expenses to see AI suggestions.
2. **Batch Posting**: On Essentials and higher plans, review and post multiple transactions marked as ready by the AI.
3. **Reconciliation**: Upload your bank statement to let the AI match transactions and highlight discrepancies.

## Payments AI
1. **Late Fees**: Set up late fee policies directly from the feed notifications.
2. **Invoice Autofill**: Copy and paste contract details or emails into the invoice autofill tool to generate invoices automatically.

## Customer AI
1. **Lead Tracking**: Connect your email to sync leads, which are scored as warm, hot, or cold.
2. **Draft Responses**: Use the AI to draft follow-up emails based on lead interactions.

## Project Management AI
1. **Project Setup**: Upload project documents to automatically generate timelines and milestones.
2. **Task Management**: On Enterprise plans, tasks are also auto-filled from project documents.

This guide provides actionable steps to maximize the utility of each AI agent within QuickBooks Online.
