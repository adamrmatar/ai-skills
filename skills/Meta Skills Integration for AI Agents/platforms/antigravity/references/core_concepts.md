# Core Concepts of Meta Skills

Meta skills are advanced capabilities that enhance the performance of AI agents across a wide range of tasks. These skills are not specific to any single workflow but rather improve the overall functionality and efficiency of AI interactions. Key meta skills include:

1. **Process Interviewer**: Helps in planning and understanding complex tasks by asking detailed questions.
2. **Prompt Master**: Optimizes prompts according to best practices for better outputs.
3. **Humanizer**: Removes signs of AI-generated writing to make text sound more human.
4. **Fact Checker**: Verifies factual claims in AI-generated content.
5. **Find Skills**: Discovers and installs skills from a vast ecosystem.
6. **Front-End Slides**: Creates high-quality, animated presentations.
7. **Decision Toolkit**: Aids in making informed decisions through systematic analysis.
8. **MCP Builder**: Builds custom connectors for software integration.

These skills collectively enhance the AI's ability to handle complex, nuanced tasks efficiently and effectively.
